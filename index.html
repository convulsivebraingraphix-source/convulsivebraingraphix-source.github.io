About Me

My name is Yareli Colin, and I'm a Los Angeles–based visual storyteller with a B.A. in Film & Television Production and complementary minors in Graphic Design and Marketing. My work blends animation, motion design, and cinematic composition to create visuals that feel expressive, intentional, and rooted in culture.

I love turning ideas into clear, emotional experiences, whether through character‑driven animation, event branding, or narrative design. I approach every piece with a focus on mood, clarity, and emotional impact—whether I'm crafting a 4‑second brand animation, developing visual assets for campus events, or building narrative-driven portfolio pieces that highlight my growth across the production pipeline.

My recent work blends animation, motion design, and visual storytelling to create pieces that feel both expressive and intentional. From short-form animated sequences and motion graphics to character-driven storyboards and event branding, each project reflects my love for cinematic composition, cultural storytelling, and thoughtful design.
